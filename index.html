Why Join AETHER?


AETHER is mostly an OSRS based community with members from all over the world. the clan mainly focuses on PvM content and Raids, offering 'learner raids' for those who want the help to learn, but also doing a small amount of PvP content aswell. We hold monthly events with GP prizes and theres always plenty of random events being held by our clan members to offer more prizes to the community. However more importantly, we are a friendly community that welcomes anybody who wants to join!
